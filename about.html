From a non-IT background to leading QA teams—here’s how I built a fulfilling career in software testing and automation.

🌱 Humble Beginnings

My journey began in science, inspired by my mother’s belief that “Education is the most powerful gift you can give yourself.” I completed a Master’s in Inorganic Chemistry, but after moving to Pune post-marriage, I pivoted toward tech—driven by curiosity and opportunity.

With my husband’s support, I pursued a computer course and later an MBA. During the Y2K era, I entered IT as a developer, but the emerging QA role resonated deeply. Testing gave me not just a career—it gave me purpose.

“Sometimes the best paths are the ones we never planned. Testing found me—and I found myself in it.”

Over time, I embraced automation, mastering tools like Selenium, Cypress, and Playwright. When automation was booming, many believed testers would be replaced by developers. That didn’t happen.

Now, with the rise of AI, the same fear resurfaces. But here’s what I’ve learned:

“AI won’t replace testers. But testers who embrace AI will replace those who don’t.”

I started with no formal IT background—and I made it. So why not you? If you're curious, committed, and willing to learn, the path is open.

My story isn’t just mine—it’s proof that with the right mindset, anyone can thrive in tech. Let’s build your journey together.

🧠 Learning the Craft

I began with manual testing, mastering test design, edge cases, and user empathy. Over time, I transitioned into automation using Selenium, Java, and TypeScript, discovering the power of precision and repeatability.

🛠️ Automation & Framework Architecture

I architected scalable frameworks using Playwright, Cypress, and Selenium. My focus: modular design, CI/CD integration, and maintainability—ensuring automation supports long-term quality.

☁️ Cloud QA & Mobile Testing

I embraced platforms like BrowserStack and SauceLabs to scale testing across devices and browsers. I led mobile automation efforts, simulating real-world conditions and ensuring seamless user experiences.

🤝 Mentorship & Leadership

As a QA Lead, I’ve mentored freshers, guided teams, and built resources that empower others. Helping others grow has been one of the most fulfilling parts of my journey—turning potential into confidence.

🔮 Future Vision

I’m passionate about AI-driven testing, cloud platforms, and mobile testing at scale. The future of QA lies in blending human insight with intelligent automation—and I’m committed to shaping that future.

“You don’t need a tech degree to thrive in QA—you need curiosity, courage, and consistency.”
