🚀 My QA Journey

Early Days

My journey into Quality Assurance started with curiosity — a drive to understand how systems fail and how to make them better. I quickly realized that testing was more than just finding bugs; it was about building confidence in software.

Mastering Automation

Over the years, I transitioned from manual testing into automation, learning tools like Selenium, Cypress, and Playwright. I built frameworks that reduced testing effort and increased reliability, making QA a core part of the delivery pipeline.

Mentorship & Leadership

As a QA Lead, I’ve been fortunate to mentor freshers and guide teams. Helping others grow in their careers has been one of the most rewarding parts of my journey.

Future Vision

Today, I’m deeply interested in AI-driven testing, cloud platforms like BrowserStack/SauceLabs, and mobile testing at scale. I believe the future of QA lies in blending human insight with intelligent automation.
