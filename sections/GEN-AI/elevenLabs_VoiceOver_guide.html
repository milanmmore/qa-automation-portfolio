🎙️ AI Voice-Overs with ElevenLabs: Step-by-Step Guide

🟢 Step 1: Create Your ElevenLabs Account

  • Visit ElevenLabs and sign up.
  • Use email, Google, or GitHub to register.
  • Start with the free plan to explore features.

Source: Frintle Guide

🧭 Step 2: Explore the Dashboard

  • Navigate Speech Synthesis, VoiceLab, Projects, Settings.
  • Most voice-over work happens in Speech Synthesis and VoiceLab.

Source: ConceptBeans Guide

🎤 Step 3: Choose or Create a Voice

  • Use Pre-made Voices: Select from stock voices in Speech Synthesis.
  • Create Custom Voice: Use VoiceLab → “Create Voice” → Adjust settings → Save.

Source: Toolify Guide

📝 Step 4: Enter Your Script

  • Paste your text into the Speech Synthesis editor.
  • Choose voice, language, style, and emotional tone.
  • Adjust pacing, pauses, and pronunciation.

Video: Full Tutorial

🔊 Step 5: Generate and Preview Audio

  • Click “Generate” to synthesize voice-over.
  • Preview and tweak settings if needed.
  • Download audio as MP3 or WAV.

Video: Best AI Voice Generator

🧬 Step 6: Clone Your Voice (Optional)

  • Go to VoiceLab → Upload a sample of your voice.
  • ElevenLabs creates a synthetic version of your voice.
  • Use it for personalized voice-overs.

Video: Voice Cloning Tutorial

🎬 Step 7: Add Voice to Video or Project

  • Use audio in your video editor, podcast, or app.
  • Repeat with different voices for multi-speaker projects.

Video: Voice-Over Studio Tutorial
